About This Website

How This Website Began

As a self-proclaimed traveler and foodie, I love traveling and trying new foods, but grocery shopping in a foreign country can be overwhelming. Different languages, unfamiliar brands, and unclear ingredients make it difficult to know what is popular or safe to eat.

The International Snack Explorer was created to help travelers, students abroad, and curious food lovers discover well-known snacks from selected countries using real data from the Open Food Facts API.

The Problem

When visiting or living in another country, it can be hard to figure out:

  • Which snacks or drinks are well-known or commonly purchased
  • What allergens or dietary restrictions apply
  • How to read ingredient lists in another language
  • What local brands and flavors exist

To simplify popularity, this project uses the unique number of barcode scans (unique_scans_n) from Open Food Facts. This is a straightforward, measurable way to highlight the top 5 snacks in each country.

Who This Helps

  • Travelers WHo are looking to try popular local foods
  • International Students Who are adapting to new grocery stores
  • People with Dietary Restrictions Who need clear allergen info
  • New Residents Who are learning what brands are common
  • Food Lovers Who are curious about global snacks

About the API

The Open Food Facts API contains millions of food products from around the world. It provides:

  • Product Names and Brands
  • Ingredients and Allergen Details
  • Nutritional Information
  • Categories and Tags
  • Popularity Based on Barcode Scans
